This is a wearable jewelry & headwear prototype inspired by the ornate curves and floral motifs of Rococo architecture. Designed digitally using 3D modeling, the headpiece and matching hand accessory explore elegance and asymmetry through form and flow. While the entire piece remains at the prototype stage, it was modeled with 3D printing in mind, particularly for the crown and roses. The pearl pendant chains were also tested through physical beading, blending digital fabrication with traditional handcraft techniques.
