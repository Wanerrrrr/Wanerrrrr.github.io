HELLO !

My name is Waner Li.

I am a designer who focuses on design and technology. I am very passionate about exploring the combination of design and technology, as I believe merging aesthetic elements that express emotions with standardized numerical sciences is a wonderful thing. My work embodies interdisciplinary elements to the fullest. The medium I use the most is games. In creating each game, I handle everything from plotting the storyline to artistic design, coding, and audio. Artistic design is a major focus for me, involving 3D modeling, animation, lighting, UI design, and VFXs. Beyond games, my work extends to various aspects of digital media, including AR experience, web design and animation, as well as graphic design, such as digital posters and illustrations. Additionally, I am also involved in wearable art design, both in analog and digital forms.
